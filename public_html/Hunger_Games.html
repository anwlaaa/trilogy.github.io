The Girl On Fire

In order to escape the Hunger Games, Katniss must overcome several lethal ordeals, negotiate intricate personal relationships, and learn to influence how others view her.

I VOLUNTEER AS TRIBUTE!

Katniss must influence others' impressions of her in order to achieve the best strategic advantage possible, then learn to survive inside the arena after volunteering to take her sister's place in the Hunger Games.

After outlasting the other tributes, Katniss and Peeta threaten to commit suicide rather than battle after a rule change transforms them into enemies.

Despite the fact that Katniss and Peeta won the Hunger Games, she must strive to appease the Capitol, which is furious with Katniss for threatening suicide and forcing them to make a decision they didn't like.

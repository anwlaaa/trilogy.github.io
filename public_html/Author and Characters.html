About The Author: Suzanne Collins

Suzanne Collins, the author of The Hunger Games Trilogy, began her professional career in 1991. Working on Nickelodeon shows and writing a variety of stories. The Hunger Games Trilogy is a worldwide best-seller. Since its release in September 2008, the trilogy has spent more than six years to date on The New York Times bestseller list.


About The Main Characters


The Boy With The Bread

Peeta Mellark is a male tribute who represents District 12 with Katniss Everdeen. During the games, Katniss becomes his main ally and soul mate. He's smart, artistic, really compassionate, and a talented visual artist in Katniss' eyes.

The Girl On Fire

Katniss Everdeen is a powerful, independent, resourceful sixteen-year-old with a maturity much above her years. Her family, which consists of her mother and a younger sister named Prim, depends on her for financial support. She learned how to hunt and forage from her father before he died in a mine explosion.

The Drunk Coach

Haymitch Abernathy is the only Hunger Games survivor from District 12. Throughout the Games, Haymitch is Katniss and Peeta's coach. He devises a plan to play up Katniss and Peeta's unconditional love, allowing both of them to survive although typically only one winner is allowed.

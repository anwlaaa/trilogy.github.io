"Remember who the real enemy is."

Katniss tries to keep her loved ones and herself safe as an undercurrent of rebellion rises in Panem, and she finds herself fighting for her life once more in the 75th Hunger Games, having enraged the Capitol at the end of the previous novel.

Katniss contends with President Snow's threats while attempting to avoid inflaming emotions in the districts, and she learns about an underground revolt spreading across Panem.

Katniss helps tear down the force field in the arena after understanding there is a plot involving her and Peeta set in action by the other tributes.

Katniss discovers the entire facts of a massive scheme to destroy the Capitol, for which she has unwittingly become a symbol, and finally comprehends the significance of several mysterious incidents.
